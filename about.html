Our Story

Saucier was born from a passion for transforming ordinary meals into extraordinary culinary experiences.

Founded in 2020 in Tripoli, Lebanon, we set out to create gourmet sauces that would elevate home cooking to restaurant-quality standards. Our team of chefs combines traditional Lebanese flavors with international techniques to craft unique, versatile sauces.

Each of our sauces is made with the finest locally-sourced ingredients and prepared in small batches to ensure quality and freshness.

Our Mission

We believe that great food starts with great ingredients. Our mission is to:

  • Create authentic, high-quality sauces
  • Support local farmers and producers
  • Make gourmet cooking accessible to everyone
  • Preserve traditional Lebanese flavors
